  1. Do not buy everything
  2. Get a fruit early game
  3. Do not fight bosses that are higher level than you
  4. Do not fight players with a coat,title or a name and numbers above their head
  5. Do not fight players that have higher bounty than you
  6. Accept quests
  7. Do not try to go to islands which are not recommended
  8. If you click the compass on your left side of you screen,you will be directed to your recommended quest
  9. Team up if you want to survive
  10. Do not be a marine be a pirate
  11. Get a smoke fruit or any elemental fruit
  12. When you level up you get stat points. Put them in melee,defense,blox fruit(if you have a fruit) or sword(if you have a sword)never gun
  13. Do not trade in first sea with a random person only in second sea with the cafe table and the third sea with the turtle mansion tables
  14. For level 50 and above players before rolling a fruit,get some one to hit you then reset your character
  15. Do the sabre quest.If you are a rubber fruit user, you are immune to the thunder god boss,rumble and quake fruit users
  16. If you are a quake or rumble fruit user do not go near a rubber fruit user a rubber fruit user can stretch their arms and legs but pole v2 can damage them
  17. You get pole v1 from the thunder god
  18. The fruits that can fly are 'Ghost,Magma,Light,Sound,Phoenix,Dragon(transformed), Shadow,Venom'
  19. There is a NPC called the blox fruit dealer which sells you fruits there are new fruits every 4 hours
  20. Do not buy fast boats get the portal fruit instead and grind until you have mastery 200 to get world warp
